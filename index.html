About Research Talks Teaching

I'm a PhD student at the Institute of Philosophy (ISFI) of the University of Italian Switzerland (USI) in Lugano. I work in Logic and the Philosophy of Mathematics; in particular notions related to metamathematics and arithemtization.

My PhD project is supervised by Prof. A. Giordani and co-supervised by Prof. V. Halbach. It is funded by my SNF Doc.CH Grant 227030 entitled Intensionality in Metamathematics and Gödel's Second Incompleteness Theorem (see here for details).

I obtained my master's degree from the University of the Italian Switzerland (USI) in 2022 and my bachelor's degree from the University of Geneva (UNIGE) in 2020.
